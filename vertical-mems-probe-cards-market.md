The growing complexity of integrated circuits and the need for precise electrical measurements during the testing phase are boosting the adoption of vertical MEMS probe cards. Additionally, the expanding applications of IoT and 5G technology are fueling the demand for advanced packaging solutions, further driving the market growth.</p><p>Another trend in the vertical MEMS probe cards market is the development of miniaturized and flexible designs.
