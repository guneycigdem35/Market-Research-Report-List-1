<p><ul><li>Hot Air Drying Equipment</li><li>Heated Surface Drying Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial dryers for agricultural products market consist of two main types: hot air drying equipment and heated surface drying equipment. Hot air drying equipment uses heated air to remove moisture from agricultural products, such as grains or fruits. It operates by blowing hot air over the product, causing the moisture to evaporate. On the other hand, heated surface drying equipment involves using heated surfaces to remove moisture through direct contact with the agricultural products. Both types of equipment are commonly used in the agricultural industry to efficiently and effectively dry agricultural products.</p></p>
