<p><ul><li>Apolipoprotein A1 Kits</li><li>Apolipoprotein B Kits</li><li>Apolipoprotein E Kits</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Apolipoprotein test kits refer to diagnostic tools used to measure the levels of specific types of apolipoproteins in the blood. These test kits are categorized into different market types based on the specific apolipoproteins they measure. Apolipoprotein A1 kits measure the levels of apolipoprotein A1, which is primarily involved in high-density lipoprotein (HDL) metabolism. Apolipoprotein B kits measure apolipoprotein B levels, a major component of low-density lipoprotein (LDL) particles. Apolipoprotein E kits measure apolipoprotein E levels, which play a role in lipid transport and metabolism. Lastly, "Others" category represents kits that measure different apolipoproteins not covered by the previous types.</p></p>
