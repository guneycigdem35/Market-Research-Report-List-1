<p><strong>The Precision Air Entrainment Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Column Type</li><li>Pressure Gauge Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Precision air entrainment meters are used to measure the amount of air entrainment admixture in concrete mixes. The Water Column Type of meters utilize a water-filled tube that measures the air content by the difference in water level. On the other hand, the Pressure Gauge Type relies on a pressure gauge to determine the air content based on the pressure increase when air bubbles are introduced into the mixture. Both types are commonly used in the construction industry to ensure the desired air content in concrete mixes for optimal performance.</p></p>
