<p><p>The self-propelled aerial device market includes various types of equipment used for reaching high altitudes safely. Telescoping boom lifts are devices with extendable arms, allowing operators to access tall areas with precision. Articulated boom lifts have multiple hinged sections, enabling them to reach over obstacles. Scissor lifts consist of a platform and folding supports, enabling vertical movement. Spider lifts are compact and flexible machines with multiple joints. Lastly, "others" may include specialty lifts like cherry pickers or towable booms. Each type has unique features catering to different needs for elevated work.</p></p>
