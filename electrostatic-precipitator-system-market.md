<p><strong>The Electrostatic Precipitator System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet ESP</li><li>Dry ESP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrostatic precipitator system market consists of two main types: Wet ESP and Dry ESP. </p><p>Wet ESP, also known as Wet Electrostatic Precipitator, employs the use of water or other liquid in the collection plates. This liquid helps to enhance the particle collection process by increasing the particle size, thus resulting in better removal efficiency.</p><p>On the other hand, Dry ESP uses an electric field to charge particles, causing them to stick to the collection plates. This type of system does not require the use of liquids and is more suitable for certain applications where water usage is not desirable or possible.</p><p>Both Wet ESP and Dry ESP provide effective solutions for removing airborne particles in various industries to ensure cleaner air quality.</p></p>
