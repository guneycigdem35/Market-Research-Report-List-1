<p><p>The Angiographic Interventional Therapy System market types include X-ray Angiography, CT Angiography, Magnetic Resonance Angiography (MRA), and MR-CT Angiography. X-ray Angiography uses X-rays and contrast dye to visualize blood vessels. CT Angiography combines CT scanning with contrast dye to produce detailed images of blood vessels. Magnetic Resonance Angiography uses magnetic fields and radio waves to create images of blood vessels. MR-CT Angiography is a combination of magnetic resonance and CT angiography, providing both anatomical and functional information about blood vessels.</p></p>
