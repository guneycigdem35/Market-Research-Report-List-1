<p><p>Sterile surgical catgut refers to an absorbable suture material used in surgical procedures. Catgut is made from collagen, usually sourced from the submucosa of sheep intestines. It is available in various types, including plain catgut and chromic catgut. Plain catgut is untreated and absorbs quickly in the body, while chromic catgut undergoes a process that makes it resistant to absorption, allowing for prolonged healing time. Both types are commonly used in surgeries to close wounds and are known for their biocompatibility and ease of handling.</p></p>
