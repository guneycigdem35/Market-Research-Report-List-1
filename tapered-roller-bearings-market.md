<p><p>The tapered roller bearings market is categorized into different types: single-row tapered roller bearings, double-row tapered roller bearings, four-row tapered roller bearings, and others. Single-row tapered roller bearings have a single row of rollers and are commonly used in applications requiring high radial and thrust load capacities. Double-row tapered roller bearings have two rows of rollers and are suitable for heavy-duty applications. Four-row tapered roller bearings have four rows of rollers and are mainly used in steel mill equipment. Other types of tapered roller bearings include inch series bearings and non-standard series bearings.</p></p>
