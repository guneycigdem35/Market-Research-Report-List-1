<p><p>Cystatin C testing is a diagnostic method used to measure the levels of cystatin C in the blood. Cystatin C is a protein produced by the cells in the body and is filtered by the kidneys. It serves as a reliable marker for renal function as it is freely filtered by the glomeruli and almost completely reabsorbed by the tubules. Unlike creatinine, cystatin C levels are not affected by age, sex, muscle mass, or diet, making it a more accurate measure of kidney function. Cystatin C testing is particularly useful in detecting early kidney dysfunction, monitoring the progression of chronic kidney disease, and predicting the risk of future renal complications.</p><p>The Cystatin C Testing Market is anticipated to exhibit steady growth during the forecast period.
