<p><strong>The Boat Anchor Chain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Studless Anchor Chain</li><li>Studlink Anchor Chain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The boat anchor chain market can be classified into two main types: studless anchor chain and studlink anchor chain. Studless anchor chain is a type of chain that is typically used in small to medium-sized boats. It is characterized by its lack of protruding studs, which allows for easy handling and smooth operation. On the other hand, studlink anchor chain is designed for larger vessels and features protruding studs that help to increase the strength and stability of the chain. Both types of chains are essential for anchoring boats securely and ensuring their safety during mooring.</p></p>
