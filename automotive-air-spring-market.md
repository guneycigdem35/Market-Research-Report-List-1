<p><strong>The Automotive Air Spring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capsule Air Spring</li><li>Membrane Air Spring</li><li>Compound Air Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive air springs are classified into three main types based on their construction and design: Capsule air spring, Membrane air spring, and Compound air spring. Capsule air springs feature a cylindrical shape with a single bellows, whereas membrane air springs have a flat design with a single convoluted membrane. Compound air springs combine elements of both capsule and membrane designs to provide a balance of durability and performance. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of load capacity, ride comfort, and overall longevity in various automotive applications.</p></p>
