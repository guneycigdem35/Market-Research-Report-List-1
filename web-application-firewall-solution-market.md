<p><p>Web Application Firewall (WAF) solutions are designed to protect web applications from cyber threats, such as malicious attacks or data breaches. The market for WAF solutions can be classified into two main types: On-Premises WAF and Cloud-Based WAF. </p><p>On-Premises WAF solutions are installed and operated within an organization's own infrastructure. They provide greater control and customization options but require dedicated hardware, software, and maintenance.</p><p>In contrast, Cloud-Based WAF solutions are hosted and managed by a third-party provider. They offer scalability, flexibility, and centralized management, eliminating the need for hardware and software setup.</p><p>Both types of WAF solutions aim to safeguard web applications, but they differ in their deployment and management approaches.</p></p>
