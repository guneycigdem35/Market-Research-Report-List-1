<p><ul><li>Dry Sprinkler Systems</li><li>Dry chemical suppression systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry extinguishing system market includes two major types: dry sprinkler systems and dry chemical suppression systems. Dry sprinkler systems are designed to control and extinguish fire by releasing water only when a fire is detected and activated, preventing accidental water damage. On the other hand, dry chemical suppression systems utilize chemicals, such as powder or foam, to suppress fires by interrupting the chemical reactions of the fire. Both systems are widely used in various industries and settings to provide effective fire protection measures.</p></p>
