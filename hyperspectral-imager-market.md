<p><p>A hyperspectral imager is a type of imaging system that collects and processes information across a wide range of electromagnetic spectrum, providing detailed spectral and spatial information. The increasing demand for accurate and reliable remote sensing solutions for environmental monitoring, agriculture, defense and surveillance, mineral exploration, and healthcare is driving the market growth. Additionally, the rising adoption of hyperspectral imaging in industries such as food and agriculture, pharmaceuticals, geology, and forestry is fueling market expansion.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the hyperspectral imager market is the integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms.
