<p><p>The LED embedded glass market comprises two main types: flat LED embedded glass and curved LED embedded glass. Flat LED embedded glass refers to glass panels that have embedded LED lights and maintain a flat surface. These panels are commonly used in applications such as display screens and smart windows. On the other hand, curved LED embedded glass refers to glass panels that have embedded LED lights and are designed with a curved surface. These panels are often used in applications that require curved display screens, such as TVs and automotive dashboards.</p></p>
