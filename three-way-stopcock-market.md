<p><p>The three-way stopcock market consists of various types, with two common variations being the slant-hole three-way stopcock and T-shaped three-way stopcock. The slant-hole stopcock features a slanted design that allows for precise control of fluid flow. On the other hand, the T-shaped stopcock has a straightforward design with three ports in a T formation, enabling multiple connections for fluid administration or withdrawal. Both types offer versatility and convenience in medical settings, making them popular choices among healthcare professionals.</p></p>
