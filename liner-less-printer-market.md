<p><p>A linerless printer is a type of thermal printer that does not require a liner or backing material for printing. It directly prints the label onto the substrate, typically using thermal transfer technology. These printers are used in various applications like packaging, retail, logistics, and transportation.</p><p>The linerless printer market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. One of the key factors driving the market growth is the increasing demand for sustainable packaging solutions. Linerless printing eliminates the need for liner waste, making it a more environmentally friendly option. Additionally, linerless printing reduces the overall operational cost by eliminating the need for liner waste recycling and storage.</p><p>Another trend driving the market growth is the increasing adoption of linerless labels by various industries. Linerless labels offer benefits like increased print area, improved productivity, and reduced material costs. These advantages have led to their widespread adoption in industries such as food and beverage, healthcare, and retail.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in linerless printing technology have further propelled market growth.
