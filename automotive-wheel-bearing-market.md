<p><strong>The Automotive Wheel Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ball</li><li>Tapered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wheel bearing market consists of two types: ball bearings and tapered roller bearings. Ball bearings are small metallic spheres that allow for smooth rotation between the wheel and axle. They are compact and provide low friction, making them suitable for high-speed applications. Tapered roller bearings, on the other hand, have conical rollers that taper towards the ends, allowing them to handle both radial and axial loads. They provide increased durability and can withstand heavy loads, making them ideal for truck and SUV applications.</p></p>
