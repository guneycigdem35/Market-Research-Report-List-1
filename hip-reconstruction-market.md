<p><strong>The Hip Reconstruction Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Cemented Hip Replacement</li><li>Primary Cementless Hip Replacement</li><li>Partial Hip Replacement</li><li>Revision Hip Replacement</li><li>Hip Resurfacing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hip reconstruction market consists of various types of procedures aimed at treating hip conditions. Primary cemented hip replacement involves fixing an artificial joint to the existing bone using cement. Primary cementless hip replacement involves securing an implant without cement, relying on bone growth for stability. Partial hip replacement replaces only the damaged part of the joint. Revision hip replacement replaces a previously implanted artificial joint. Hip resurfacing involves reshaping the damaged hip joint, preserving most of the bone. These procedures address different aspects of hip conditions and aim to improve mobility and reduce pain.</p></p>
