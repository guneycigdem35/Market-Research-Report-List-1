<p><p>The fluoroscopy equipment market consists of two main types: fluoroscopy devices and C-arms. Fluoroscopy devices are imaging systems that use X-rays to produce real-time images of a patient's internal structures. These devices are typically used in a variety of medical procedures, such as diagnosis, surgeries, and interventions. C-arms, on the other hand, are a specific type of fluoroscopy device that have a C-shaped arm that can be maneuvered around the patient. C-arms are commonly used in surgical and interventional procedures to provide real-time imaging guidance.</p></p>
