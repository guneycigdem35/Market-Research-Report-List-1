<p><p>Whole-house humidifiers are available in two main types: warm mist humidifiers and cool mist humidifiers. Warm mist humidifiers work by heating water to create steam, which is then released into the air to add moisture. These types of humidifiers are effective at killing bacteria and viruses in the air. In contrast, cool mist humidifiers disperse cool water vapor into the air without heating it first. They are typically more energy efficient and are ideal for households with children or pets, as they do not involve the risk of burns.</p></p>
