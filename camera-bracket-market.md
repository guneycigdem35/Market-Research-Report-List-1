<p><p>The camera bracket market consists of three main types: wall-mounted, floor-standing, and ceiling type brackets. Wall-mounted brackets are designed to be attached to walls and offer stability and flexibility in mounting cameras. Floor-standing brackets are suitable for situations where wall-mounting is impractical, as they allow cameras to be securely placed on the floor. Ceiling type brackets are specifically designed to be mounted on ceilings, providing an elevated view and optimal coverage. These different types cater to various installation needs and enable cameras to be positioned effectively in different settings.</p></p>
