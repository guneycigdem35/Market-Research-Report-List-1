They are typically used for small-scale applications and offer high filtration efficiency. On the other hand, Cartridge Filters are larger and designed for higher capacity applications. They are more durable and require less frequent replacement compared to capsule filters. Both types of filters play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and purity of photoresist chemicals.</p></p>
