<p><p>Real-time Location Services (RTLS) Applications enable the tracking and monitoring of assets or people in real-time. Various technologies are utilized for this purpose including Wi-Fi, RFID, UWB, Ultrasound, Infrared, and ZigBee. Wi-Fi-based RTLS leverages existing Wi-Fi infrastructure to track assets. RFID uses radio frequency identification tags to identify and locate objects. UWB relies on short-range radio waves for precise location determination. Ultrasound-based RTLS utilizes sound waves outside the range of human hearing. Infrared-based systems use infrared signals for accurate location tracking. ZigBee is a low-power wireless technology specifically designed for RTLS applications.</p></p>
