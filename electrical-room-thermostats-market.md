<p><strong>The Electrical Room Thermostats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Millivolt Thermostats</li><li>24 Volt Thermostats</li><li>Line Voltage Thermostats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical room thermostat market can be categorized into three types: millivolt thermostats, 24 volt thermostats, and line voltage thermostats. Millivolt thermostats are typically used in systems that generate low voltage, such as gas fireplaces or wall heaters. 24-volt thermostats are commonly found in residential heating and cooling systems that use a central heating unit and air conditioning system. Line voltage thermostats are used in systems that operate on high voltage, like electric baseboard heaters or radiant floor heating systems. Each type serves a specific purpose in controlling temperature within electrical rooms.</p></p>
