<p><p>Sealless pumps, also known as magnetically driven pumps or magnetic drive pumps, are a type of centrifugal pump that do not require a traditional shaft seal for its operation. Instead, they use a magnetic coupling to transmit power from the motor to the impeller, eliminating the need for a mechanical seal and minimizing leakage risks.</p><p>The sealless pumps market has been experiencing steady growth in recent years. Factors driving this growth include the increasing demand for leak-free pumping solutions across various industries, such as chemicals, pharmaceuticals, oil and gas, and water treatment. The ability of sealless pumps to handle corrosive and hazardous fluids safely makes them a preferred choice in these industries.</p><p>Additionally, stringent environmental regulations and the need for improved energy efficiency have also contributed to the market growth.
