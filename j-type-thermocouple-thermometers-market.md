<p><p>J-Type Thermocouple Thermometers are available in three market types: single input, dual input, and multiple input. </p><p>Single input thermometers can only measure the temperature of one object at a time. They are commonly used for basic temperature measurement tasks.</p><p>Dual input thermometers have the capability to simultaneously measure the temperature of two objects. This is useful for comparison or differential temperature monitoring.</p><p>Multiple input thermometers can measure the temperature of multiple objects simultaneously. They are often used in industrial applications where multiple temperature measurements are required for process control or monitoring purposes.</p></p>
