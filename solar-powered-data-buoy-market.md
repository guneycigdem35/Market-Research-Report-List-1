<p><ul><li>Base</li><li>Tower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar powered data buoy market includes two main types: base market and tower market. In the base market, data buoys are anchored to the seabed or lake bed, utilizing a weighted base to ensure stability. These buoys are suitable for shallow water applications and are commonly used for environmental monitoring or research purposes. On the other hand, the tower market involves data buoys that are mounted on a tower structure above the water surface. These buoys are typically employed in deeper waters and serve various purposes such as weather forecasting, oceanography, and offshore energy operations.</p></p>
