<p><strong>The Electromagnetic Shock Absorber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pure Active Shock Absorber</li><li>Semi-Active Shock Absorber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electromagnetic shock absorber market consists of two main types: pure active shock absorbers and semi-active shock absorbers. Pure active shock absorbers utilize electromagnetic coils to actively adjust damping forces in real-time based on road conditions and vehicle dynamics. This type provides superior control and performance but is more expensive. On the other hand, semi-active shock absorbers use a mixture of passive and active technologies. They can adjust damping forces within a limited range, offering a balance between performance and cost. These shock absorbers are commonly used in automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
