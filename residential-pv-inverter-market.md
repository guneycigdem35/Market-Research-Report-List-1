<p><p>The Residential PV Inverter market consists of various types of inverters, including String Inverters, Micro Inverters, and Others. String Inverters are commonly used in residential solar installations and operate by connecting multiple solar panels in a series, converting the DC electricity generated into AC electricity. On the other hand, Micro Inverters are installed on each individual solar panel, allowing them to work independently and optimizing energy production. The "Others" category refers to alternative inverter technologies used in residential PV systems that may include power optimizers, hybrid inverters, or solar energy storage systems.</p></p>
