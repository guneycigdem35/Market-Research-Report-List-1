<p><p>The hot riveting tools market can be categorized into three main types: pneumatic riveting tools, battery operated riveting tools, and hand operated riveting tools. Pneumatic riveting tools are powered by compressed air and offer high efficiency and precision in riveting applications. Battery operated riveting tools, on the other hand, provide flexibility and portability as they are not reliant on a power source. Hand operated riveting tools are manually operated and are suitable for small-scale riveting tasks. Each type offers its own advantages and caters to different user requirements in various industries.</p></p>
