<p><strong>The Baby Incubators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transport Baby Incubator</li><li>Normal Baby Incubator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Baby incubators are specialized medical devices used to provide a controlled environment for newborns in intensive care. Transport baby incubators are designed for safe transportation of infants between hospitals or emergency situations, ensuring continuous monitoring and support during transit. Normal baby incubators are stationary units used within hospitals' neonatal care units to create a stable environment for premature or critically ill babies. Both types aim to provide necessary warmth, humidity, and oxygen levels while monitoring vital signs, but transport incubators prioritize mobility for safe transportation, while normal incubators focus on long-term care within healthcare facilities.</p></p>
