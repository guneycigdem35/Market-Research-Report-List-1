<p><p>The commercial mist cooling system market consists of three main types: low pressure, medium pressure, and high pressure systems. </p><p>Low pressure mist cooling systems release fine mist at a pressure of about 40-100 psi, making them suitable for small outdoor areas, such as patios or gardens. </p><p>Medium pressure mist cooling systems operate at a pressure of 100-250 psi, covering larger areas like restaurants and warehouses, providing efficient cooling. </p><p>High pressure mist cooling systems offer the most powerful cooling effect, with pressures over 250 psi, being used in places with extreme heat like amusement parks or sports stadiums, cooling large crowds effectively.</p></p>
