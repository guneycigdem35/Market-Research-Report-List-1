<p><ul><li>Float Glass</li><li>Rolled Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optically clear glass is available in two different market types: float glass and rolled glass. Float glass is produced by pouring molten glass onto a bed of molten tin, which results in a uniform and smooth surface. It is widely used in architectural applications, such as windows and glass facades. Rolled glass, on the other hand, is made by passing molten glass through a pair of rollers, giving it a textured surface. It is commonly used in the automotive industry for windows and windshields.</p></p>
