<p><p>Insulation Monitoring Devices (IMDs) are crucial in ensuring the safety and reliability of electrical systems. There are two main types of IMDs based on the type of electrical system they are designed for: AC Type and DC Type. AC Type IMDs are used in alternating current systems, while DC Type IMDs are used in direct current systems. Both types monitor insulation resistance and provide alerts in case of any faults, helping prevent electrical hazards and ensuring smooth operation of the system.</p></p>
