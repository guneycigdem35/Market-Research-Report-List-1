Medium-level order pickers can reach heights between 3m and 8m, enabling operators to access multiple levels. High-level order pickers are designed for more extensive operations and can lift loads beyond 8m, maximizing vertical storage capacity in large facilities. These different market types cater to various needs in the materials handling industry.</p></p>
