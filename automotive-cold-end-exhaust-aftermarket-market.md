<p><p>The automotive cold-end exhaust aftermarket can be categorized into two main types: basic cold-end exhaust and performance cold-end exhaust. The basic cold-end exhaust refers to the replacement of the original factory exhaust system with a similar, standard aftermarket exhaust system. It aims to provide a direct replacement option that performs at a similar level to the original system. On the other hand, the performance cold-end exhaust focuses on enhancing the vehicle's performance, often by increasing horsepower and improving exhaust flow. It may involve the use of high-performance materials and design modifications to optimize the exhaust system's efficiency.</p></p>
