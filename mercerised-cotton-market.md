Yarn mercerization involves treating the cotton yarn with caustic soda solution to strengthen the fibers and give them a lustrous appearance. Knit mercerization refers to the process of mercerizing cotton knit fabrics, which enhances their strength, color vibrancy, and sheen. Cloth mercerization entails treating cotton cloth with caustic soda under controlled conditions, resulting in improved fabric properties such as increased strength, enhanced dye receptivity, and a soft feel. These different market types cater to various industries, including textile manufacturing and fashion.</p></p>
