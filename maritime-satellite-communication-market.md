<p><ul><li>Very Small Aperture Terminal (VSAT)</li><li>Mobile Satellite Services (MSS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Maritime Satellite Communication market consists of two types: Very Small Aperture Terminal (VSAT) and Mobile Satellite Services (MSS). VSAT is a technology that provides high-speed internet and voice communication via satellite to ships at sea. It offers reliable and continuous connectivity, enabling various applications like video conferencing and browsing. On the other hand, MSS provides satellite-based communication services through handheld devices, allowing maritime users to access voice and data services even in remote areas where terrestrial networks are unavailable. MSS is widely used for emergency and distress communications.</p></p>
