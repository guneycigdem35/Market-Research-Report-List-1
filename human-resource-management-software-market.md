<p><p>The Human Resource Management Software market can be categorized into two types: Cloud-based and On-premises. Cloud-based HRMS is a web-hosted software solution that allows organizations to access and manage HR functions remotely via the internet. This type offers flexibility, scalability, and ease of use. On the other hand, On-premises HRMS is installed and maintained within the organization's infrastructure. It provides better control over data but requires substantial upfront investments and IT expertise for maintenance. Both types cater to the HR management needs of organizations but differ in deployment and management approach.</p></p>
