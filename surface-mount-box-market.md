<p><p>The Surface Mount Box market consists of two types: Single Port and Multiport. Single Port surface mount boxes are designed to accommodate one network connection or cable, making them suitable for individual applications. On the other hand, Multiport surface mount boxes can accommodate multiple network connections or cables, making them ideal for installations that require multiple connections in a single location. Both types of surface mount boxes cater to the needs of different users, depending on the number of connections required for their specific networking requirements.</p></p>
