<p><p>The Laparoscopic Pump market is categorized into three main types: Single Chamber, Dual Chamber, and Other. </p><p>The Single Chamber laparoscopic pump is equipped with a single reservoir that stores and delivers gas to create a pneumoperitoneum during laparoscopic surgeries. </p><p>The Dual Chamber laparoscopic pump consists of two separate reservoirs, one for gas insufflation and the other for fluid irrigation, providing better control and efficiency during surgical procedures. </p><p>The Other category includes various types of laparoscopic pumps that may have additional features, such as built-in pressure sensing or advanced fluid management capabilities.</p></p>
