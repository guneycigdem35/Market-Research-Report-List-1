<p><p>Ultrasonic scalpels are surgical devices that use ultrasonic energy to cut, coagulate, dissect, and remove tissues during surgical procedures. These scalpels consist of an ultrasonic generator, a handpiece, and disposable or reusable tips. The ultrasonic energy produced by the generator is converted into mechanical vibrations by the handpiece, which are then transferred to the tips, resulting in precise and controlled tissue cutting.</p><p>The global ultrasonic scalpels market is expected to have significant growth in the forecast period. The increasing prevalence of chronic diseases requiring surgical interventions, such as cardiovascular diseases, cancer, and neurological disorders, is driving the demand for ultrasonic scalpels.
