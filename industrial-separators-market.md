<p><p>Industrial separators are used to separate different materials or substances in various industries. Centrifugal separators use centrifugal force to separate solids from liquids or gases. Magnetic separators use magnets to remove unwanted metallic contaminants from materials. Cyclone separators use rotational motion to separate particles from a gas stream. Gas-liquid separators separate gas and liquid phases in a pipeline. Liquid-liquid separators separate two immiscible liquids. Other types of separators include gravity separators, electrostatic separators, and membrane separators, which are used in specific applications based on the separation requirements.</p></p>
