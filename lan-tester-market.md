<p><p>A LAN tester is a device used to test and troubleshoot Local Area Networks (LANs) to ensure their proper functioning. LAN testers can identify wiring faults, such as open circuits, shorts, miswires, and split pairs, allowing network administrators to quickly locate and fix network issues.</p><p>The LAN tester market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for high-speed and reliable network connections, especially in the corporate sector, is one of the key factors driving market growth. Additionally, the rapid growth of the IT and telecommunications industry is further fueling the demand for LAN testers. The emergence of technologies like IoT, 5G, and cloud computing is also contributing to the market growth, as they require robust LAN infrastructure for seamless connectivity.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing adoption of LAN testers in the residential sector is expected to provide lucrative growth opportunities for market players. Additionally, the market is witnessing several technological advancements, such as the integration of LAN testers with advanced features like Wi-Fi scanning, PoE testing, and crosstalk testing.</p><p>The latest market trends in the LAN tester market include the development of portable and handheld LAN testers, which offer convenience and ease of use for technicians working on network installations and maintenance.
