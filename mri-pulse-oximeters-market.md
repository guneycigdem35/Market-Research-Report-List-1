<p><p>The MRI Pulse Oximeters market includes two types: Wired MRI Pulse Oximeters and Wireless MRI Pulse Oximeters. Wired MRI Pulse Oximeters require a physical connection to the monitoring device, providing accurate and real-time measurements of a patient's oxygen saturation level during an MRI scan.
