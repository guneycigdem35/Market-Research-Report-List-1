<p><p>The lawn mower switches market consists of different types of switches used in lawn mowers. Speed switches allow users to adjust the mowing speed according to their preference. Trigger switches are handheld switches that activate the lawn mower when pressed. Rocker switches are toggles that can be flipped back and forth to turn the lawn mower on or off.
