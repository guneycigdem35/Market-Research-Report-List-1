<p><p>Anemometer probes are categorized into three main types based on the speed range they are designed to measure - low speed, medium speed, and high speed. Low speed anemometer probes are used to measure air flow at slower velocities typically found in HVAC systems. Medium speed probes are used for airflow measurement in industrial processes and ventilation systems. High speed probes are designed for measuring fast airflows in applications such as aerodynamics testing and environmental monitoring. Each type of probe is specifically tailored to accurately gauge air speed within its designated range.</p></p>
