<p><p>Helium leak test systems are used to detect and locate leaks in various products and equipment. The market for these systems can be categorized into three types: single chambered, double chambered, and multi-chambered. </p><p>Single chambered systems have one chamber where the test is conducted. This type is suitable for small-scale testing and simpler products. </p><p>Double chambered systems have two separate chambers for testing and loading/unloading products. It allows for quicker testing and higher throughput.</p><p>Multi-chambered systems have multiple chambers that can simultaneously test multiple products, resulting in even higher production rates and efficiency.</p></p>
