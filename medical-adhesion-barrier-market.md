<p><ul><li>Film Formulation</li><li>Gel Formulation</li><li>Liquid Formulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical adhesion barrier market consists of three main types: film formulation, gel formulation, and liquid formulation. Film formulation adhesion barriers are thin sheets that can be easily applied to surgical sites to reduce the risk of adhesions. Gel formulation adhesion barriers are thick, gel-like substances that can be applied directly to tissues during surgery. Liquid formulation adhesion barriers are fluid solutions that can be sprayed or irrigated onto tissues. These different formulations provide surgeons with options to prevent tissue adhesion and minimize post-operative complications.</p></p>
