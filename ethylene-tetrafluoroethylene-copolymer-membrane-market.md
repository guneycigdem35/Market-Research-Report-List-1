<p><p>Ethylene-tetrafluoroethylene copolymer membrane is available in different types based on thickness - Below 200 µm, 200-300 µm, and Above 300 µm. The Below 200 µm membranes are typically used for applications requiring high gas permeability and selectivity. The 200-300 µm membranes offer a balance between gas permeability and mechanical strength. The Above 300 µm membranes are known for their high mechanical strength and durability, making them suitable for harsh environments or high-pressure applications.</p></p>
