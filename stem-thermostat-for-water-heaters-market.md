<p><p>The stem thermostat for water heaters is available in two types: single-phase stem and multi-phase stem. Single-phase stem thermostats are designed for water heaters that operate using a single electrical phase. They regulate the water temperature by opening and closing the electrical circuit as necessary. On the other hand, multi-phase stem thermostats are used for water heaters that require multiple electrical phases. They are designed to handle the complex power requirements of such systems. Both types of stem thermostats play a crucial role in regulating and maintaining the desired water temperature in water heaters.</p></p>
