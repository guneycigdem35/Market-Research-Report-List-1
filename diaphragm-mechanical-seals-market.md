<p><strong>The Diaphragm Mechanical Seals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pusher Type Seal</li><li>Non-pusher Seal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diaphragm mechanical seals are categorized into two main types: pusher type seals and non-pusher seals. Pusher type seals use springs or bellows to maintain contact between the sealing faces, while non-pusher seals rely on diaphragm tension to accomplish the same task. Pusher type seals are preferred for applications with higher operating speeds and pressures, while non-pusher seals are favored for their simplicity and reliability. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring leak-free operation in various industrial equipment and machinery.</p></p>
