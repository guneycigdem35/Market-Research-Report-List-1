<p><ul><li>Single-Channel Cable Equaliser</li><li>Multi-Channel Cable Equaliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adaptive equalizer market is divided into two main types: single-channel cable equalizers and multi-channel cable equalizers. Single-channel cable equalizers are designed to compensate for the loss and distortion of a single channel signal over long cable distances. They help to improve the overall signal quality and prevent data corruption. On the other hand, multi-channel cable equalizers are capable of correcting multiple channels simultaneously, making them ideal for applications that involve transmitting multiple signals over long distances. Both types of equalizers play a crucial role in ensuring reliable data transmission and maintaining signal integrity.</p></p>
