This method involves directing high-frequency sound waves into the material, which then bounce back and are analyzed to determine the material's thickness and potential corrosion.</p><p>The Ultrasonic Corrosion Detection Market is experiencing significant growth due to several factors. Firstly, the increasing need for preventive maintenance and asset integrity management in industries such as oil and gas, power generation, and aerospace is driving the demand for reliable corrosion detection techniques.
