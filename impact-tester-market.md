<p><p>The Impact Tester market consists of various types, including the Charpy Test, Izod Test, and other related markets. The Charpy Test assesses the impact resistance of materials by measuring the energy absorbed when a notched sample is subjected to breaking forces. The Izod Test, on the other hand, measures the impact resistance of materials by evaluating the energy required to break a notched sample supported at one end. Apart from these well-known tests, other markets in the Impact Tester industry include tests for dynamic fracture toughness, drop-weight impact, and more, which enable the evaluation of material behavior under different impact conditions.</p></p>
