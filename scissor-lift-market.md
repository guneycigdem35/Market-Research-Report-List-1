Manufacturers are integrating advanced safety features such as anti-tipping systems, overload sensors, and automatic leveling mechanisms into their products to enhance operator safety and improve productivity. <p><p>Scissor lift market types are categorized into Class 1, Class 2, and Class 3 based on their intended use and characteristics. Class 1 scissor lifts are mainly designed for indoor use on smooth surfaces. They offer lower lifting heights and are suitable for lighter applications. Class 2 scissor lifts are versatile and designed for both indoor and outdoor use on uneven surfaces. They can handle heavier loads and provide higher lifting heights. Class 3 scissor lifts are specifically designed for rough terrain outdoor use, capable of handling heavy loads and offering the highest lifting heights.</p></p>
