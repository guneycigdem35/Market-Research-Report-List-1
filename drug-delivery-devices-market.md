<p><strong>The Drug Delivery Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injection type</li><li>Mucosal type</li><li>Implanted type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drug delivery devices market includes three key types of devices: injection type, mucosal type, and implanted type. Injection type devices administer drugs directly into the patient's body through subcutaneous, intramuscular, or intravenous routes. Mucosal type devices deliver medications through the nose, mouth, or other mucosal membranes, ensuring rapid absorption. Implanted type devices are surgically placed in the patient's body to dispense drugs over a prolonged period. These distinct types cater to different medical conditions and provide efficient drug delivery methods to ensure effective treatment outcomes.</p></p>
