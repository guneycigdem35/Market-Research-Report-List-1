<p><p>Uncooled infrared imaging equipment refers to devices used to detect and capture thermal radiation in various spectrums. The market types include near infrared, shortwave infrared, mid-wave infrared, and long-wave infrared. </p><p>Near infrared refers to the spectrum closest to visible light and is used in applications such as night vision and surveillance. Shortwave infrared covers a broader spectrum and is utilized in various fields like chemical analysis and moisture detection. Mid-wave infrared captures thermal radiation emitted by objects and finds application in areas like thermography and industrial inspections. Long-wave infrared focuses on the longest wavelengths and is commonly used in thermal imaging for medical diagnostics, security monitoring, and search and rescue operations.</p></p>
