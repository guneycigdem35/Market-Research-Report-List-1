<p><p>The school attendance software market can be divided into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based attendance software operates on remote servers and is accessible through the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and reduced infrastructure costs. On the other hand, on-premises attendance software is installed and hosted locally within the school's network, providing full control over data and security. Both options have their own advantages and considerations, with cloud-based solutions prioritizing convenience and accessibility, whereas on-premises solutions focus on data control and customization.</p></p>
