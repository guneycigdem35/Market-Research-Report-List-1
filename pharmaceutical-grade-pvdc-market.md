<p><p>The pharmaceutical grade PVDC market can be divided into two major types: PVDC resins and PVDC latex. PVDC resins refer to solid forms of PVDC that are commonly used for coating pharmaceutical products, providing excellent moisture barrier properties. On the other hand, PVDC latex is a liquid form of PVDC that is often used for coating materials such as films and foils, offering high heat resistance and chemical stability. Both PVDC resins and PVDC latex play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and effectiveness of pharmaceutical packaging and preservation.</p></p>
