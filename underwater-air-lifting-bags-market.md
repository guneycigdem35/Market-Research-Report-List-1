Firstly, the increasing number of underwater construction activities, such as the installation of offshore wind farms and oil platforms, is driving the demand for these lifting bags. Additionally, the growing trend of marine salvage and wreck removal operations is further boosting market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the rising emphasis on safety in underwater operations and the need for efficient and cost-effective solutions are also contributing to the market expansion.
