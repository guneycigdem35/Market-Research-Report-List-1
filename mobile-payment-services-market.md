<p><p>Mobile payment services can be categorized into two main types: remote and proximity payments. Remote payments refer to transactions conducted through mobile devices without the need for physical contact between the customer and the payment terminal. This includes online purchases, app-based payments, and mobile banking. On the other hand, proximity payments involve using mobile devices to make transactions in physical locations by proximity to the payment terminal, such as tapping a phone at a contactless payment terminal or using near field communication (NFC) technology. These types of payments are commonly used in stores, restaurants, and public transportation.</p></p>
