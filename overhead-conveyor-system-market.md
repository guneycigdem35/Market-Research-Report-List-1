<p><p>The overhead conveyor system market consists of two main types: Automatic and Semi-Automatic. Automatic systems require little to no human intervention as they are fully mechanized and controlled by computer systems. They are efficient and can handle higher volumes of material or products. On the other hand, semi-automatic systems involve a combination of manual and automated processes. They may require human operators for loading or unloading, while the conveyor system takes care of the transportation. These systems offer flexibility and are suitable for medium-sized operations.</p></p>
