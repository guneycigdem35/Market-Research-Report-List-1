<p><ul><li>Plated Film Lens</li><li>Synthesis Film Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiation protection glasses are divided into two main market types: plated film lens and synthesis film lens. Plated film lens glasses are made by depositing a thin layer of metal or metal oxide onto the lens surface, providing protection against radiation. On the other hand, synthesis film lens glasses are manufactured by combining different materials using specific techniques to create the required radiation shielding properties. Both types of glasses aim to reduce the harmful effects of radiation and ensure safety for individuals working in radiation-prone environments.</p></p>
