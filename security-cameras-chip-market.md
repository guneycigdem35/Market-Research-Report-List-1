<p><ul><li>IPS Chip</li><li>SoC Chip</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The security camera chip market consists of various types, including IPS chips, SoC chips, and others. IPS (Image Sensor Processor) chips are responsible for processing the image data captured by the camera sensor, enhancing image quality and enabling features like high dynamic range (HDR). SoC (System-on-Chip) chips integrate multiple functions like image processing, video encoding, and network connectivity onto a single chip, offering enhanced performance and power efficiency. The "other" category in this market would include different types of chips designed for specific purposes such as image recognition, AI processing, or low-light imaging.</p></p>
