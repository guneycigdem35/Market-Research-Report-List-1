<p><p>The moisture detector market consists of two main types: handheld and fixed detectors. Handheld moisture detectors are portable devices used to measure and detect moisture levels in various materials such as wood, concrete, or soil, making them ideal for construction and DIY applications. On the other hand, fixed moisture detectors are permanently installed in buildings or facilities and continuously monitor moisture levels to prevent mold growth, structural damage, or other moisture-related issues. Both types serve the purpose of detecting and preventing moisture-related problems but differ in terms of portability and installation.</p></p>
