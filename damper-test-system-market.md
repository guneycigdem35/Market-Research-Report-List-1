<p><ul><li>Magnetorheological Damper</li><li>Hydraulic Damper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Damper Test System market consists of two main types: Magnetorheological Dampers and Hydraulic Dampers. Magnetorheological Dampers use a magnetic field to adjust the viscosity of the fluid inside the damper, allowing for precise and rapid control of damping characteristics. On the other hand, Hydraulic Dampers use hydraulic fluid to absorb and dissipate energy, providing effective vibration damping. Both types of dampers are widely used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction for improving vehicle stability, comfort, and safety.</p></p>
