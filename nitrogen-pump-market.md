<p><p>The nitrogen pump market is categorized into two major types: triplex pump and quintuplex pump. A triplex pump is a type of positive displacement pump that has three cylinders, which enables it to deliver high-pressure nitrogen for various applications. On the other hand, a quintuplex pump has five cylinders, allowing for even higher pressure output. Both types of pumps are widely used in industries such as oil and gas, industrial cleaning, and chemical processing for nitrogen injection and transfer operations.</p></p>
