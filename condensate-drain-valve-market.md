<p><strong>The Condensate Drain Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fully Automatic</li><li>Semi-Automatic</li><li>Manual</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The condensate drain valve market is divided into three types: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Fully automatic drain valves are equipped with sensors that automatically detect water and open the valve to drain it. Semi-automatic valves require manual intervention to initiate the draining process, but the valve itself handles the rest. Manual drain valves, on the other hand, require complete manual operation to initiate and control the draining process. These different types cater to various preferences and requirements of consumers based on their desired level of automation in the condensate drainage process.</p></p>
