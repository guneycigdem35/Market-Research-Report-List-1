Nanoemulsions find applications in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, food and beverages, and agriculture.</p><p>The market for nanoemulsions is experiencing significant growth due to their numerous advantages over conventional emulsions. Nanoemulsions offer better bioavailability of drugs, improved texture and appearance in cosmetic products, extended shelf-life in food products, and enhanced solubilization and encapsulation properties.
