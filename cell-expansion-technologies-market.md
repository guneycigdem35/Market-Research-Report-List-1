<p><p>Cell Expansion Technologies refers to the methods and tools used to grow and expand cells in a laboratory setting. The market for these technologies can be categorized into four main types: reagents, media, sera, and disposables. </p><p>Reagents are substances used to facilitate cell growth by providing essential nutrients and factors. Media refers to the liquids or gels where cells are grown, providing a suitable environment for their expansion. Sera are blood-derived products that contain growth factors necessary for cell proliferation. Disposables include various consumables, such as culture vessels and pipettes, used during cell expansion processes. These different types of products collectively support the efficient and successful growth of cells.</p></p>
