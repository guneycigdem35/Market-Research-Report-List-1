<p><p>An Externally Mounted Ultrasonic Level Switch is a type of level sensor used for determining the level of liquids or solids in tanks or vessels. It works by emitting ultrasonic waves that bounce off the surface of the material being measured, with a level switch triggering once a specific level is reached. These level switches are preferred for their ease of installation, minimal maintenance requirements, and ability to work with a wide range of materials.</p><p>The Externally Mounted Ultrasonic Level Switch Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.9% during the forecast period.
