<p><p>An angiography catheter is a medical device used to perform angiography, a procedure that allows doctors to examine the blood vessels for any blockages or abnormalities. It is a thin, flexible tube that is inserted into a blood vessel through a small incision. The catheter is guided through the blood vessels to the area of interest, and a contrast agent is injected to make the blood vessels visible on X-ray images.</p><p>The angiography catheter market has witnessed significant growth in recent years due to the rising prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, increasing geriatric population, and technological advancements in catheter design. According to the latest market research, the angiography catheter market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the angiography catheter market is the development of innovative catheter designs. Manufacturers are focusing on developing catheters with improved flexibility, trackability, and pushability. These advancements allow for better navigation through complex anatomy, reducing the risk of complications and improving patient outcomes.</p><p>Another emerging trend in the market is the increasing adoption of minimally invasive procedures. Minimally invasive angiography procedures using catheters offer several advantages such as reduced hospital stays, shorter recovery times, and lower risk of infection. As a result, there is a growing demand for angiography catheters from healthcare facilities and patients.</p><p>In addition, the rising demand for angiography procedures in emerging economies is also contributing to the market growth. Developing countries such as China, India, and Brazil are witnessing an increase in the number of patients with cardiovascular diseases due to lifestyle changes and an aging population.</p><p>Overall, the angiography catheter market is expected to continue its growth trajectory due to the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, technological advancements, and the adoption of minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>

<p><p>The angiography catheter market includes various types, including RDC type, SHK type, and Others. RDC type catheters are designed to access peripheral and coronary arteries, offering precise navigation and easy handling. SHK type catheters are specifically developed for selective coronary angiography, with an ergonomic shape allowing for smooth guide wire handling. The Others category encompasses different catheter types tailored for specific patient needs or procedures not covered by the aforementioned types. These catheters are crucial in achieving accurate visualization and diagnosis during angiography procedures.</p></p>
