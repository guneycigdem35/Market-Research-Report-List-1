<p><ul><li>Linear Position Sensor</li><li>Rotary Position Sensor</li><li>Proximity Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crankshaft position sensor market includes three types of sensors: linear position sensor, rotary position sensor, and proximity sensors. Linear position sensors are used to measure straight-line movement and provide accurate position feedback. Rotary position sensors measure rotary movement and provide data on the angle or the position of a rotating object. Proximity sensors detect the presence or absence of an object within a certain range without physical contact. These sensors play a crucial role in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, where precise position detection is required.</p></p>
