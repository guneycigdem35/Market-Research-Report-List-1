<p><p>Optical absolute encoders are devices used for measuring the position or rotation angle of a shaft in various industries. Singleturn encoders provide position feedback in a single revolution, while multiturn encoders offer feedback for multiple revolutions. Singleturn encoders are commonly used in applications where a complete rotation is essential, such as robotics. Multiturn encoders, on the other hand, are ideal for applications where numerous rotations need to be measured accurately, such as in heavy machinery or industrial equipment. Both types of encoders employ optical technology for precise position detection.</p></p>
