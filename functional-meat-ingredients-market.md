<p><p>Functional meat ingredients can be categorized into various types such as hydrocolloids, cereal flours, proteins from animal sources, proteins from vegetable sources, and vegetable fibers. Hydrocolloids are used for texture improvement, moisture retention, and stabilization in meat products. Cereal flours are added for binding and bulking purposes. Proteins from animal sources enhance protein content and functionality in meat products, while proteins from vegetable sources provide plant-based alternatives. Vegetable fibers are used for their water-binding and textural properties in meat products.</p></p>
