<p><strong>The Hydronic Balancing Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Balancing Valve</li><li>Dynamic Balancing Valve</li><li>PICV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydronic balancing valves market consists of various types including static balancing valves, dynamic balancing valves, and pressure independent control valves (PICV). Static balancing valves ensure a constant flow rate by adjusting the valve opening manually based on the design flow. Dynamic balancing valves use a differential pressure regulator to automatically maintain a constant flow rate. PICVs combine the functions of a balancing valve and a control valve, maintaining a constant flow rate while also providing precise temperature control.</p></p>
