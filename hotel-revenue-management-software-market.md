<p><strong>The Hotel Revenue Management Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud Based</li><li>On-Premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hotel revenue management software market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software operates on the internet, allowing hoteliers to access and manage data remotely from any device with an internet connection. This type offers scalability, lower upfront costs, automatic updates, and easy integration. On the other hand, on-premises software is installed on the hotel's local servers and requires physical maintenance and IT support. While offering more control and customization, it entails higher upfront costs and limited accessibility compared to cloud-based solutions.</p></p>
