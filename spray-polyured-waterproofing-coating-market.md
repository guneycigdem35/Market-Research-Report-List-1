<p><p>Spray Polyurea Waterproofing Coating is a market that consists of two main types: Pure Polyurea Coating and Hybrid Polyurea Coating. Pure Polyurea Coating is a 100% solids, fast-curing, and flexible coating that provides excellent water resistance. It is commonly used in applications that require high-performance waterproofing. On the other hand, Hybrid Polyurea Coating is a blend of polyurethane and polyurea, combining the best properties of both materials. This type of coating offers enhanced durability, flexibility, and chemical resistance, making it suitable for various waterproofing projects.</p></p>
