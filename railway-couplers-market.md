<p><p>Railway couplers are devices used to connect railway vehicles for safe and efficient train operations. There are three main types of couplers in the market: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic couplers. </p><p>Manual couplers require manual intervention for connecting and disconnecting vehicles. Semi-automatic couplers involve some level of automation, reducing the manual effort needed. Automatic couplers are fully automated, connecting and disconnecting vehicles without human intervention. </p><p>Each type offers varying levels of efficiency, safety, and ease of use, allowing railway operators to choose the couplers that best suit their specific requirements and operational needs.</p></p>
