<p><strong>The Duplicator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Duplicators</li><li>Digital Duplicators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical duplicators refer to old-fashioned duplication machines that use mechanical components to produce multiple copies of a document. These machines are manually operated and use ink or carbon paper for reproduction. On the other hand, digital duplicators are modern devices that use digital technology to duplicate documents. They can reproduce large quantities of documents quickly and efficiently, offering features like high-quality printing, fast processing, and networking capabilities. Unlike mechanical duplicators, digital duplicators do not require manual operation and offer improved efficiency and convenience in large-scale copying tasks.</p></p>
