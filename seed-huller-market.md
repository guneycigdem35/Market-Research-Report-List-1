<p><p>The seed huller market includes various types of seed hullers used for removing the hulls from seeds. Disc hullers are a type of seed huller that use rotating abrasive discs to strip away the hulls from the seeds. They are often used for larger seeds and have adjustable settings to control the hulling process. Impact hullers, on the other hand, use mechanical impact to separate the hulls from the seeds. They are usually used for smaller seeds and are known for their high efficiency in hulling.</p></p>
