<p><p>The Oil & Gas Subsea Umbilicals, Risers & Flowlines (SURF) market consists of three main components - umbilicals, risers, and flowlines. Umbilicals are cables used to supply power, control, and communication between surface facilities and subsea equipment. Risers are vertical pipes that transport produced fluids from the seabed to surface facilities. Flowlines are pipelines that transport oil and gas between subsea wells and production facilities. Together, these components form the essential infrastructure for subsea oil and gas production operations.</p></p>
