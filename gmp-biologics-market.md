<p><ul><li>Monoclonal Antibodies</li><li>Polyclonal Antibody</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GMP Biologics market comprises two main types: Monoclonal Antibodies and Polyclonal Antibodies. Monoclonal Antibodies are artificially created, identical antibodies that target specific antigens and are commonly used in the treatment of various diseases. Polyclonal Antibodies, on the other hand, are derived from different B cells and recognize multiple antigens. Both types play a crucial role in the field of biotechnology and pharmaceuticals, offering diverse applications in diagnostics, therapeutics, and research. They are manufactured following Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) guidelines to ensure their quality, safety, and efficacy.</p></p>
