<p><p>Dry yogurt is a popular powdered form of yogurt, available in various market types. Skimmed dry yogurt refers to a yogurt with low-fat content, achieved by removing the cream during processing. All three market types offer consumers options to choose yogurt based on their dietary preferences and needs.</p></p>
