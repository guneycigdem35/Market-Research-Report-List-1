<p><ul><li>On-line UPS System</li><li>Standby UPS System</li><li>On-line Interactive UPS System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Uninterrupted Power Supply (UPS) Systems market comprises three main types: On-line UPS System, Standby UPS System, and On-line Interactive UPS System. On-line UPS Systems provide continuous power by converting AC power to DC and then back to AC. Standby UPS Systems switch to battery power when the main power source fails. On-line Interactive UPS Systems combine features of both previous types, providing continuous power while also storing and using energy when needed. These systems play a crucial role in ensuring uninterrupted power supply for various industries and sectors.</p></p>
