<p><p>Actuator cables are used to transmit electrical signals between actuators and control systems in various industries. There are two types of actuator cables: elbow and straight. Elbow actuator cables have a bent shape, enabling them to connect to actuators in tight spaces or corners. On the other hand, straight actuator cables have a linear design and are used when there is a direct connection between the actuator and control system. Both types are essential in the proper functioning of actuators, allowing for efficient communication and control within a system or machinery.</p></p>
