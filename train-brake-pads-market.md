Composite brake pads are made by combining different materials for enhanced performance and reduced noise. Powder metallurgy brake pads are manufactured using powdered metals, resulting in high strength and heat resistance. Cast iron brake pads are made from cast iron material, offering exceptional durability and heat dissipation capabilities.
