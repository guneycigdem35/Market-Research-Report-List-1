<p><p>Flare tips are used in industrial sites to safely combust waste gases. Open pipe flare tips are simple and cost-effective, allowing for the direct release of gases. Air-assisted flare tips inject air into the waste gas stream to enhance combustion efficiency. Coanda flare tips use a curved design to redirect the gases, creating a stable flame. The "Others" category includes various types of flare tips, such as sonic flare tips, which rely on high-velocity gas to achieve combustion. These different market types offer distinct advantages based on factors like cost, efficiency, and safety.</p></p>
