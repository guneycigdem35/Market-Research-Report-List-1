<p><p>Micro-bypass systems are devices that are used in surgical procedures to divert blood flow away from a specific area of the body, allowing the surgeon to work on that area without interfering with the blood flow. These devices are commonly used in cardiac and vascular surgeries, as well as in neurovascular and orthopedic procedures. The micro-bypass system works by redirecting the blood flow through an artificial pathway, bypassing the area where the surgery is being performed.</p><p>The global micro-bypass systems market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
