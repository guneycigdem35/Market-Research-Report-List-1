<p><p>The Automatic Hermetic Door Market includes two types: Single Leaf and Double Leaves doors. Single Leaf doors consist of one door panel that opens and closes automatically, providing a hermetic seal. It is suitable for smaller spaces or areas with limited passage requirements. On the other hand, Double Leaves doors consist of two door panels that open and close automatically, providing a larger passage width. This type is ideal for larger spaces or areas with higher traffic flow demands. Both types offer the convenience of automation and ensure a tight seal when closed for enhanced hygiene and safety.</p></p>
