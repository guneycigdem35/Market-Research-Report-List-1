<p><p>The electronic voltage stabilizer market is divided into two main types: AC voltage stabilizers and DC voltage stabilizers. AC voltage stabilizers are designed to regulate and stabilize alternating current voltage before it is supplied to electrical appliances or devices. They ensure a consistent and safe voltage level, protecting appliances from overvoltage or undervoltage fluctuations. On the other hand, DC voltage stabilizers are used to stabilize and regulate direct current power sources, providing a constant and reliable voltage output for devices that run on DC power.</p></p>
