<p><p>Globe and check valves are types of flow control devices used in various industries to regulate the flow of fluids through pipes. </p><p>A globe valve consists of a movable disk or plug that moves perpendicular to the flow of the fluid, allowing precise control of the flow rate. It is commonly used in applications that require throttling, such as in water treatment plants, oil and gas industries, and power generation plants.</p><p>On the other hand, a check valve is designed to prevent backflow, allowing the fluid to flow in only one direction. It automatically closes to prevent reverse flow when the pressure drops or changes direction. Check valves are widely used in plumbing systems, wastewater treatment plants, and various industrial applications.</p><p>The global globe and check valves market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years. This growth can be attributed to several factors, including the increasing demand for energy, growing industrialization, and the need for effective fluid control systems.
