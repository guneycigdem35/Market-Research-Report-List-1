<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>PCI Bridge Chips are used in various electronic devices to connect different types of PCI buses. There are three types: PCI to PCI, PCIe to PCI, and PCI to DSP.</p><p>1. PCI to PCI: In this market type, the bridge chip enables communication between two PCI buses. It allows data transfer and ensures compatibility between different PCI devices.</p><p>2. PCIe to PCI: This market involves bridge chips that facilitate communication between PCI Express (PCIe) and PCI buses. It enables integration of PCIe devices with legacy PCI systems.</p><p>3. PCI to DSP: In this market, the bridge chip connects PCI buses with Digital Signal Processors (DSPs). It enables efficient data transfer and control between these two components.</p></p>
