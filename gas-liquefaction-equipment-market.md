<p><p>The gas liquefaction equipment market consists of three main types: heat exchangers, compressors, and others. Heat exchangers play a crucial role in the liquefaction process by transferring heat from the gas to a coolant, facilitating the conversion of the gas into a liquid state. Compressors are responsible for pressurizing the gas, reducing its volume, and creating the conditions necessary for liquefaction. The "others" category includes various equipment such as distillation columns, condensers, and fractionation units that aid in the gas liquefaction process by performing additional functions like separation and purification.</p></p>
