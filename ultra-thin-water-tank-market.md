This type is commonly used in boats and ships to promote stability. On the other hand, a water tank without bezel does not have any internal compartments. It provides a larger, uninterrupted space for holding water or other liquids. This type is often utilized in residential or commercial settings where space is limited or aesthetics are prioritized.</p></p>
