<p><p>Engineering Design Software is a vast market that can be broadly categorized into two types: Computer-Aided Design (CAD) and Electronic Design Automation (EDA) Software. CAD software is used for creating and modifying 2D or 3D designs, assisting engineers in visualizing and simulating their ideas. On the other hand, EDA software is specifically designed to facilitate the development of complex electronic systems, such as integrated circuits and printed circuit boards, by automating various design tasks, including verification, testing, and synthesis. Both types of software play a crucial role in enhancing productivity and efficiency in engineering design processes.</p></p>
