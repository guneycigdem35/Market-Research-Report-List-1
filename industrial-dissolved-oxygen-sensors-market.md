<p><ul><li>Optical Dissolved Oxygen Sensor</li><li>Electrochemical Dissolved Oxygen Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial dissolved oxygen sensors market includes two main types: optical dissolved oxygen sensors and electrochemical dissolved oxygen sensors. Optical dissolved oxygen sensors measure the oxygen content by using a fluorescent dye that emits light when excited by a light source. Electrochemical dissolved oxygen sensors, on the other hand, operate by utilizing a chemical reaction to measure the oxygen content in a solution. Both types of sensors are commonly used in industrial processes to monitor and control oxygen levels, ensuring optimal conditions for various applications.</p></p>
