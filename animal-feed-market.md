<p><p>The animal feed market consists of three main types: compound feed, fodder, and forage. Compound feed refers to a mixture of various ingredients, including grains, protein sources, and additives, designed to provide balanced nutrition to different types of animals. Fodder, on the other hand, refers to dried or fresh plant material, such as hay or straw, used as feed for livestock. Lastly, forage refers to grazing crops or grasses in the field, consumed directly by animals. These three market segments play a crucial role in providing essential nutrition to animals in various forms.</p></p>
