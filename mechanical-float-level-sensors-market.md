<p><p>Mechanical float level sensors can be classified into two main market types: switch type and continuous type. Switch type sensors provide a simple on/off signal when the liquid level reaches a certain point, making them suitable for applications where precise level control is not necessary. Continuous type sensors, on the other hand, provide real-time monitoring of liquid levels, making them ideal for applications where accurate and continuous level measurement is required. Both types of sensors have their unique advantages and can be used in various industrial settings.</p></p>
