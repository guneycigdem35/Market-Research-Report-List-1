<p><strong>The Mobile Operating Tables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Electric</li><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Electro-hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile operating tables market comprises four types: manual, electric, hydraulic, and electro-hydraulic. Manual tables do not rely on any external power source and are adjusted manually. Electric tables are powered by electricity and offer convenient adjustments through a control panel. Hydraulic tables use hydraulic systems to adjust height and position. Electro-hydraulic tables combine both electric and hydraulic mechanisms, providing enhanced flexibility and precision in positioning patients during surgical procedures. These market types cater to different needs and preferences of medical professionals, ensuring efficient and comfortable operations.</p></p>
