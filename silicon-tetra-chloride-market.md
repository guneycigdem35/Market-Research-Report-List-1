Ferrosilicon is made by reducing silica with coke or charcoal in the presence of iron, and it is primarily used in steel production as a deoxidizing agent. Silicon carbide is a compound of silicon and carbon, known for its high hardness and thermal conductivity, making it ideal for abrasive applications.
