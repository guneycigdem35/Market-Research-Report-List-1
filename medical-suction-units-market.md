<p><p>Medical suction units are devices used to remove fluids, secretions, or debris from a patient's airway or surgical site. There are three main types of suction units available in the market. Electrical powered suction units operate using electricity and are commonly used in medical facilities. Vacuum powered suction units rely on an existing vacuum source to generate suction, while compressed air powered suction units use compressed air as a power source. Permanent drainages refer to systems installed within the patient's body for continuous fluid drainage. These units play a crucial role in maintaining a patient's airway and ensuring a safe surgical environment.</p></p>
