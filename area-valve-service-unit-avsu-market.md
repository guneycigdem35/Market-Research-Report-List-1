<p><p>The Area Valve Service Unit (AVSU) is a device used in the heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems to control and regulate the flow of air in a building. The AVSU is an integral part of the building automation system as it helps in maintaining the desired comfort levels and energy efficiency.</p><p>The AVSU market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for energy-efficient solutions in buildings is one of the major factors driving the market growth. The AVSU helps in optimizing energy consumption by ensuring that only the required amount of air is delivered to each area of the building. This not only reduces energy costs but also helps in reducing carbon emissions, making it an attractive solution for building owners and operators.</p><p>Moreover, the growing trend of smart buildings and the adoption of Internet of Things (IoT) in building automation systems are also fueling the market growth.
