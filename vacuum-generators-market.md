<p><ul><li>Single-Stage Vacuum Generators</li><li>Multi-Stage Vacuum Generators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vacuum generators are devices used to create a vacuum by removing gas or air from a sealed space. Single-stage vacuum generators operate through a single vacuum chamber, creating low levels of vacuum pressure. They are commonly used in applications where a moderate vacuum is required. On the other hand, multi-stage vacuum generators use multiple vacuum chambers to create greater levels of vacuum pressure. They are ideal for applications that require high levels of vacuum, such as in industrial manufacturing or scientific research.</p></p>
