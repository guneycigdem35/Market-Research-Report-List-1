<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>In-Motion Checkweighers are industrial machines used to check the weight of products while they are in motion on a production line. They are designed for high-speed and continuous weighing of items. Intermittent Checkweighers, on the other hand, weigh products when they are stationary in between intervals of movement. These machines are typically used for slower production lines or when the weighing accuracy is more important than speed. Both types of checkweighers are important in industrial settings to ensure product quality, compliance with regulations, and reduce waste.</p></p>
