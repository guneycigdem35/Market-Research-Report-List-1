<p><p>The can filling machine market can be classified into two types based on their operation: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic can filling machines are fully automated, requiring minimal human intervention. They can perform all tasks, from can placement to filling, sealing, and labeling, efficiently and continuously. On the other hand, semi-automatic can filling machines operate with some human assistance. They require manual can placement and involvement in various stages of the filling process. Both types serve the purpose of efficiently filling cans but differ in their level of automation.</p></p>
