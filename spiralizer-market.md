<p><p>The spiralizer market is comprised of two main types: manual and electric. Manual spiralizers require users to physically twist the vegetable or fruit into the blades in order to create spirals. They are affordable, portable, and often suitable for small-scale use. On the other hand, electric spiralizers have built-in motors that automate the process, providing convenience and ease of use, especially for large quantities. Although they are costlier, they offer more consistency and versatility. The choice between the two types depends on personal preferences, intended usage, and budget.</p></p>
