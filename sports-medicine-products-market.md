<p><p>The sports medicine products market includes two main types: external application and internal medicine. External application products are used externally on the body, such as braces, tapes, and wraps, to provide support, protection, and pain relief. On the other hand, internal medicine products are consumed internally, like pills, injections, and supplements, to promote healing, reduce inflammation, and enhance performance. Both these types cater to the specialized needs of athletes and individuals involved in sports activities, aiming to prevent injuries, treat ailments, and optimize performance in the field of sports medicine.</p></p>
