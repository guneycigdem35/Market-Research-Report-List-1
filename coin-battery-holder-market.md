<p><strong>The Coin Battery Holder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mount</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coin battery holder market consists of two types: surface mount and through hole. Surface mount holders are mounted directly onto the surface of a circuit board using soldering, allowing for a compact and space-efficient design. Through hole holders are mounted by inserting their metal pins through holes in the circuit board and soldering them on the other side, providing a more secure connection. Both types are used in various electronic devices to hold coin-shaped batteries, providing power in a reliable and easily replaceable manner.</p></p>
