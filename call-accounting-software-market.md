<p><p>Call accounting software is available in two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise call accounting software operates on local servers owned and maintained by the user, providing complete control over the software and data. In contrast, Cloud-Based call accounting software is hosted and managed remotely by a third-party vendor, accessible through the internet. It offers scalability, flexibility, and eliminates the need for local infrastructure. Users can access the software from anywhere, ensuring convenience and cost-effectiveness. Overall, the market types cater to different user preferences and requirements for call accounting software.</p></p>
