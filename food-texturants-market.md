<p><p>The food texturants market is composed of different types, including hydrocolloids, gums, and stabilizers. Hydrocolloids are substances that form a gel when mixed with water, providing viscosity and texture to food products. Gums, such as guar gum or xanthan gum, are used as thickeners and stabilizers, enhancing the texture and mouthfeel of food. Stabilizers, on the other hand, are ingredients that help maintain the consistency and prevent separation in food products. These texturants play a crucial role in improving the overall quality and appeal of various food items.</p></p>
