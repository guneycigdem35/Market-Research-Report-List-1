<p><p>Diverter manifolds are flow control devices used in drilling operations to direct and control the flow of drilling fluids. They are an integral part of blowout preventer systems and are primarily designed to handle high-pressure drilling environments. The purpose of diverter manifolds is to divert the drilling fluid away from the rig in case of an emergency or kick, preventing blowouts and controlling the well.</p><p>The global diverter manifolds market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for oil and gas along with the rising exploration and production activities are the major drivers for market growth.
