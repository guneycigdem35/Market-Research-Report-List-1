<p><p>The forklift truck cabins market comprises two main types: semi cabins and full cabins. Semi cabins refer to partially enclosed cabins that provide basic protection to the operator, typically featuring only the front windscreen, top cover, and safety doors. On the other hand, full cabins offer complete enclosure with doors, windows, heating, air conditioning, and other advanced features to ensure maximum safety and comfort for operators. Both semi and full cabins cater to different needs and preferences of forklift truck operators in terms of safety, weather protection, and convenience.</p></p>
