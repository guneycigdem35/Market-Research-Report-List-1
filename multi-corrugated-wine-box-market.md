<p><p>The multi corrugated wine box market consists of three main types: single wall board, double wall board, and triple wall board. Single wall board is made up of a single layer of corrugated cardboard, making it the most basic and cost-effective option. Double wall board, on the other hand, is constructed from two layers of corrugated cardboard, providing increased strength and durability. Triple wall board is the most robust option, with three layers of corrugated cardboard, offering maximum protection for wines during transportation and storage.</p></p>
