Receivers, on the other hand, receive optical signals and convert them back into electrical signals. Modulators enable the modulation of the optical signals for efficient transmission. Demodulators decode the received optical signals for further processing. These components collectively ensure the successful transmission of data through free-space optical communication.</p></p>
