<p><p>Spray booth protective film is used in the automotive industry to protect the car's surface during the painting process. There are two types of this film: solid paint resistant masking film and liquid paint resistant masking film. Solid film is a peelable, self-adhesive material that can be easily applied and removed, providing temporary protection against paint overspray. Liquid film, on the other hand, is a spray-on coating that dries to form a flexible and durable barrier against paint. Both types of film help prevent paint damage and ensure a high-quality finish.</p></p>
