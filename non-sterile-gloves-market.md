<p><p>Non-sterile gloves are disposable gloves that are not intended for use in sterile environments. The COVID-19 pandemic has further augmented the demand for non-sterile gloves, as they are essential in preventing the spread of the virus. However, the market is also witnessing certain challenges, such as the increasing preference for sterile gloves in some healthcare facilities. This could potentially hinder the growth of the non-sterile gloves market.</p><p>In terms of trends, the market is witnessing a shift towards the use of synthetic materials, such as nitrile and vinyl, instead of natural latex gloves.
