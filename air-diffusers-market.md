<p><p>Air diffusers are devices that are used to distribute air evenly in a room or building. Porous plate type diffusers have small holes on a plate that allow air to escape in a uniform pattern, creating a gentle airflow. Porous tube type diffusers consist of small tubes with tiny pores that release air in a controlled manner, creating an even distribution of airflow. Both types of diffusers are commonly used in HVAC systems to improve indoor air quality and ensure proper ventilation in various spaces.</p></p>
