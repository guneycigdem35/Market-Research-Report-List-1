<p><p>A gluten-free snack is a food item that does not contain the protein gluten. Gluten is found in grains such as wheat, barley, and rye, making it off-limits for individuals with gluten-related disorders such as celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. Gluten-free snacks are specially formulated to provide a safe and healthy alternative for people who follow a gluten-free diet.</p><p>The global gluten-free snack market has witnessed significant growth in recent years. This growth can be attributed to several factors such as the increasing prevalence of gluten-related disorders, rising awareness about the health benefits of a gluten-free diet, and the availability of a wide range of gluten-free snack options.</p><p>Market growth analysis reveals that the gluten-free snack market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 4.7% during the forecast period. Gluten-free snacks are also becoming more widely available in various retail channels, including supermarkets, specialty stores, and online platforms.</p><p>In conclusion, the gluten-free snack market is experiencing steady growth due to the increasing demand from individuals with gluten-related disorders and the growing awareness of the benefits of a gluten-free diet.
