<p><p>The Multispectral Imaging System market is divided into two main types: Single Lens and Multi-camera systems. In the Single Lens system, all the required spectral bands are captured using a single lens equipped with filters to separate different wavelengths. This system is advantageous for compactness and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, the Multi-camera system utilizes multiple cameras, each specialized in capturing a specific spectral band. This approach offers improved flexibility for image acquisition and provides higher resolution and accuracy but at a higher cost.</p></p>
