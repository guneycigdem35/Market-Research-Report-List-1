<p><p>CB radio is a type of short-range, two-way radio communication system widely used for message exchange. The CB radio market is categorized into handheld CB radios and fixed CB radios. Handheld CB radios are portable devices, ideal for personal use and communication on the go. They typically have a limited range and are commonly used by truckers, campers, and outdoor enthusiasts. Fixed CB radios, on the other hand, are designed for installation in vehicles or fixed locations. They have a higher power output and longer range, allowing for communication over larger distances. Fixed CB radios are predominantly used by truck fleets, businesses, and emergency services.</p></p>
