<p><p>The wireless video surveillance market can be categorized into two types: analog microwave transmission and digital microwave transmission. Analog microwave transmission involves the use of analog signals to transmit video data wirelessly. It is an older technology that tends to have lower image quality and limited transmission range. On the other hand, digital microwave transmission utilizes digital signals, allowing for higher video quality and longer transmission distances. It is a more advanced and reliable technology commonly used in modern wireless video surveillance systems.</p></p>
