<p><p>HJT Texturing Equipment refers to the machinery or equipment used in the manufacturing process of heterojunction (HJT) solar cells. HJT texturing equipment is designed to etch microstructures on the silicon wafer surface to increase the solar cell's efficiency.</p><p>The HJT Texturing Equipment market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
