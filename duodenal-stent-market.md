<p><p>Duodenal stents are medical devices used to treat blockages or strictures in the duodenum, a part of the small intestine. There are two main types of duodenal stents available in the market: polymer and metal duodenal stents. Polymer duodenal stents are made of biocompatible materials and are more flexible, allowing for easier placement. Metal duodenal stents, on the other hand, are made of stainless steel or nitinol and provide higher radial force to ensure better support and resistance to compression. Both types of stents help to restore proper gastrointestinal function.</p></p>
