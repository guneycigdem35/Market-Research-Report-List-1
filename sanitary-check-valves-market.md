<p><strong>The Sanitary Check Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flange Connection</li><li>Butt Welding Connection</li><li>Thread Connection</li><li>Socket Welding Connection</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Sanitary check valves are categorized based on their connection types. Flange connection check valves have flanges at the ends, allowing easy installation by bolting them onto pipework. Butt welding connection check valves are welded directly to the pipe, ensuring a secure and leak-free connection. Thread connection check valves feature threaded ends for convenient installation onto threaded pipes. Socket welding connection check valves are joined to the pipe by socket welding, which creates a strong and reliable connection. These different connection options provide flexibility and suitability for various sanitary systems.</p></p>
