<p><p>The market for AC Response Accelerometers includes two main types: Charge Mode Piezoelectric and Voltage Mode Piezoelectric accelerometers. In Charge Mode Piezoelectric accelerometers, the output generated is in the form of electric charge due to the applied force or acceleration. On the other hand, Voltage Mode Piezoelectric accelerometers produce an output in the form of voltage signal proportional to the applied force or acceleration. These accelerometers are widely used in various industries for measuring vibrations and movements, providing accurate and reliable data for analysis and control purposes.</p></p>
