<p><p>A dry box, also known as a desiccator or desiccant box, is a sealed enclosure used to store items that are sensitive to moisture and humidity. The dry box provides a controlled environment, protecting the stored items from moisture-related damage, such as rusting, molding, or degradation.</p><p>The future outlook of the dry box market is promising.
