<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Current Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall-Effect Current Sensors</li><li>Shunt Current Sensors</li><li>TMR Current Sensors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The current sensors market consists of various types. Hall-effect current sensors operate based on the Hall effect and are widely used for their low cost, high sensitivity, and non-contact measurement capabilities. Shunt current sensors measure current by detecting the voltage across a shunt resistor, offering high accuracy but requiring a physical connection. TMR (Tunneling Magnetoresistance) current sensors utilize a magnetic tunnel junction to sense the magnetic field produced by the current, providing high sensitivity and low power consumption. Additionally, there are other types of current sensors available that employ different principles for accurate current measurement.</p></p>
