<p><p>Flatbed document scanners are devices used to convert physical documents into digital files. There are two main types of flatbed scanners in the market: CCD scanners and CIS scanners. CCD scanners (Charge-Coupled Device) use a series of lenses and mirrors to capture the image of a document, providing high-quality scans with accurate color reproduction. On the other hand, CIS scanners (Contact Image Sensor) use a light source and sensors to capture the image directly, making them more compact and energy-efficient. Both types are widely used in various applications such as home offices, businesses, and government organizations.</p></p>
