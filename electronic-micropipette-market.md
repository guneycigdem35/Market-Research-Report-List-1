<p><strong>The Electronic Micropipette Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi-channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic micropipette market includes two main types: single-channel and multi-channel pipettes. Single-channel pipettes are designed to dispense and transfer small liquid volumes in a single channel at a time, providing high accuracy and precision for tasks that require precise measurements. On the other hand, multi-channel pipettes are equipped with multiple channels, allowing simultaneous dispensing or transferring of liquid into multiple wells or samples. Multi-channel pipettes excel in tasks that involve repetitive actions, increasing efficiency and saving time in high-throughput applications.</p></p>
