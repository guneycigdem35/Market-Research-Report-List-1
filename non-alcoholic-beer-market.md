<p><p>Non-alcoholic beer market types include limited fermentation and dealcoholization method. Limited fermentation non-alcoholic beer is produced by stopping the fermentation process before all the alcohol is created, resulting in a lower alcohol content. Dealcoholization method involves removing alcohol from traditionally brewed beer through methods such as vacuum distillation or reverse osmosis. Both these methods cater to consumers looking for a beer-like experience without the effects of alcohol, offering a wide variety of options in the non-alcoholic beer market.</p></p>
