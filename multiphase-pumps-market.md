<p><p>The multiphase pumps market can be classified into different types, including twin screw multiphase pumps, helico-axial multiphase pumps, and others. Twin screw multiphase pumps use two interlocking screws to handle multiphase fluids, offering high efficiency and low pulsation. Helico-axial multiphase pumps utilize a combination of centrifugal and axial flow mechanisms to handle multiphase fluids efficiently. The "Others" category includes other types of multiphase pumps that may have different designs or mechanisms. Each type of multiphase pump offers unique advantages and is designed to cater to specific application requirements in different industries.</p></p>
