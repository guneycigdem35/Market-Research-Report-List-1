<p><p>Interventional cardiovascular devices refer to medical instruments utilized in minimally invasive procedures for the treatment of heart diseases. The most commonly used interventional cardiovascular devices include stents, catheters, guidewires, and angioplasty balloons.</p><p>The market for interventional cardiovascular devices has been witnessing substantial growth in recent years.
