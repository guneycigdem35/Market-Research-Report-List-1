<p><p>The power generation engines market consists of three main types: prime power generation engines, standby power generation engines, and emergency power generation engines. Prime power generation engines are used continuously to provide a primary source of electricity, typically for remote or off-grid areas. Standby power generation engines act as a backup to the main power source when it fails or is interrupted, ensuring uninterrupted power supply. Emergency power generation engines are specifically designed to be used during emergencies, such as natural disasters or power outages, to provide immediate power.</p></p>
