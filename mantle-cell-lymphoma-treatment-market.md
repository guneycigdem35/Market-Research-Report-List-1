<p><p>Mantle cell lymphoma (MCL) is a rare, aggressive type of non-Hodgkin's lymphoma (NHL) that typically affects older adults. Targeted therapy drugs, such as ibrutinib and acalabrutinib, work by blocking specific pathways in cancer cells, inhibiting their growth.
