<p><p>ERV air exchangers are ventilation systems that provide fresh air to indoor spaces while also recovering heat or energy from the outgoing air. There are three main market types for ERV air exchangers: ceiling-mount, wall-mount, and cabinet-mount. Ceiling-mount exchangers are installed in the ceiling, allowing for efficient air distribution throughout the room. Wall-mount exchangers are mounted on walls and are suitable for spaces with limited ceiling access. Cabinet-mount exchangers are compact units that can be installed inside cabinets or closets, providing discreet ventilation solutions for smaller spaces.</p></p>
