<p><p>The High Speed Direct Attach Copper (DAC) Cable market consists of two main types: Passive DAC Cables and Active DAC Cables. Passive DAC Cables are simple copper cables that directly connect networking devices without the need for additional power or signal processing. They offer cost-effective and energy-efficient solutions for short-range communications. On the other hand, Active DAC Cables incorporate built-in chips to boost signal quality and extend transmission distances. They are suitable for long-range communications and provide enhanced performance but at a higher cost.</p></p>
