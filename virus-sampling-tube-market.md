<p><p>Virus sampling tubes are essential for collecting and transporting specimens for virus detection. Inactivated virus sampling tubes contain chemicals that deactivate the virus, making it safe for handling and transportation. These tubes are commonly used in laboratories. On the other hand, non-inactivated virus sampling tubes do not contain any deactivating chemicals and preserve the infectiousness of the samples. These tubes are typically used in research and development settings where live viruses are needed for further analysis. Both types cater to specific needs in virus detection and research.</p></p>
