<p><strong>The Pipe Relining Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cured-in-place</li><li>Pull-in-place</li><li>Pipe Bursting</li><li>Internal Pipe Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pipe relining is a method used to repair damaged or deteriorating sewer or water pipes without the need for excavation. There are several types of pipe relining techniques available. Cured-in-place relining involves inserting a flexible lining into the damaged pipe and then curing it with heat or UV light. Pull-in-place relining is a similar technique but uses a pre-measured and cut lining. Pipe bursting is a method where a new pipe is pulled through an old one, breaking it apart. Internal pipe coating involves spraying or brushing a protective coating inside the damaged pipe.</p></p>
