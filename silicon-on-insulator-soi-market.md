<p><p>Silicon-on-Insulator (SOI) is a technology that involves fabrication of integrated circuits (ICs) on a silicon wafer that is isolated from the substrate by an insulating layer. It offers numerous advantages such as improved performance, reduced power consumption, and enhanced reliability compared to traditional bulk CMOS technology. SOI provides a more efficient and cost-effective solution for the production of high-performance ICs, making it popular in the semiconductor industry.</p><p>The SOI market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years, and this trend is expected to continue in the forecast period. The growing demand for high-performance and low-power-consumption devices in various industries such as consumer electronics, automotive, and telecommunications is driving the market growth. Additionally, the increasing adoption of IoT and AI technologies is further fueling the demand for SOI-based devices.</p><p>The market is also being driven by the rising demand for smartphones and other portable devices. SOI technology enables the production of smaller and more power-efficient chips, making it ideal for use in such devices. Furthermore, the development of advanced packaging technologies and the emergence of 5G technology are expected to create lucrative opportunities for the SOI market.</p><p>In terms of trends, the market is witnessing a shift towards the adoption of FD-SOI (Fully Depleted SOI) technology. FD-SOI offers superior performance and power efficiency compared to the traditional bulk CMOS and partially depleted SOI technologies. It is being widely used in applications such as IoT, automotive, and AI, further driving the growth of the SOI market.</p><p>In conclusion, the Silicon-on-Insulator (SOI) market is experiencing steady growth and is expected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 6.8% during the forecast period. The market's growth is driven by the demand for high-performance, low-power-consumption devices in various industries, as well as the increasing adoption of IoT and AI technologies. The shift towards FD-SOI technology and advancements in packaging technologies are also contributing to the market's growth.</p></p>
