<p><p>Gas isotope analyzers are used to determine the presence and concentration of isotopes in gases. The market for these analyzers can be broadly classified into two types: radioisotope analysis and stable isotope analysis. Radioisotope analysis involves the use of radioactive isotopes to study various aspects like chemical reactions and radiocarbon dating. On the other hand, stable isotope analysis utilizes non-radioactive isotopes to study processes such as ecosystem dynamics and climate change. Both types of analyzers play a vital role in scientific research, environmental monitoring, and industrial applications.</p></p>
