<p><p>The Branch Lifter market consists of two main types: Mechanical Branch Lifter and Pneumatic Branch Lifter. Mechanical Branch Lifters utilize mechanical systems and mechanisms to lift and move branches or similar objects. They are often manually operated and provide a cost-effective solution for smaller-scale lifting needs. On the other hand, Pneumatic Branch Lifters employ compressed air as a power source to lift and manipulate branches or heavy objects. This type of lifter is known for its versatility, as it can handle heavier loads and offers greater control and precision in lifting operations.</p></p>
