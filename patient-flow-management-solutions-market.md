<p><p>Patient flow management solutions market can be categorized into two types: on-premise and in-cloud. On-premise solutions involve implementing the software within the healthcare facility's infrastructure, allowing for complete control over data storage and management. In contrast, in-cloud solutions are hosted on remote servers, accessed through the internet, and offer scalability and flexibility for healthcare providers. On-premise solutions may require higher upfront costs and maintenance, while in-cloud solutions offer cost-effectiveness and convenience, as updates and backups are handled by the service provider.</p></p>
