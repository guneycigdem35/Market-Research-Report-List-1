<p><p>The Human Resource Management Platform market can be categorized into two main types: Cloud Based and On Premises. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as it doesn't require on-site infrastructure. On the other hand, On Premises refers to a platform that is installed and managed on the company's own servers.
