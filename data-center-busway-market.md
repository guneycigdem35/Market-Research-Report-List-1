<p><p>Data Center Busway Market Types - 3-Phase 4-Wire and 3-Phase 5-Wire refer to two different electrical power distribution systems commonly used in data centers. The 3-Phase 4-Wire system consists of three phases and a neutral wire, allowing for balanced power distribution. On the other hand, the 3-Phase 5-Wire system includes an additional grounding (Earth) wire, providing enhanced safety and protection against electrical faults. Both systems are utilized to efficiently transmit electrical power within data centers, catering to different operational requirements and regulations.</p></p>
