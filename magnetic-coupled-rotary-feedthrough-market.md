<p><p>A magnetic coupled rotary feedthrough is a device used in various industrial applications to provide a hermetic seal while enabling rotary motion through a pressure barrier or vacuum chamber. It consists of two magnetically coupled components, one stationary and the other rotating, which are separated by a non-magnetic barrier. This design allows for fluid or gas to be transferred between the stationary and rotating parts without the need for mechanical seals, resulting in improved reliability and reduced maintenance.</p><p>The magnetic coupled rotary feedthrough market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. Several factors are driving this growth, including the increasing demand for reliable and efficient sealing solutions in industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, aerospace, and medical devices.
