<p><p>Orthopedic treatment of hyaluronic acid injection involves the use of hyaluronic acid-based viscosupplements to alleviate pain and improve joint function in patients with osteoarthritis. This treatment is commonly used in joints such as the knee, hip, and shoulder.</p><p>Hyaluronic acid is a natural substance found in the body that acts as a lubricant and shock absorber in the joints. In patients with osteoarthritis, the synovial fluid in the joints becomes thin and loses its lubricating properties, leading to increased friction and pain. Hyaluronic acid injections help to restore the viscosity of the synovial fluid, reducing pain and improving joint mobility.</p><p>The orthopedic treatment of hyaluronic acid injection market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
