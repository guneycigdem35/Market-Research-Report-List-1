<p><strong>The Dry-type Transformer Fan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Top Blowing Type</li><li>Side Blowing Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dry-type transformer fans are classified into two main types: top blowing and side blowing. Top blowing fans are positioned on top of the transformer and blow air downward, providing efficient cooling from the top. On the other hand, side blowing fans are installed on the side of the transformer and blow air horizontally across the transformer, allowing for effective cooling from the side. Both types of fans play a crucial role in maintaining optimal operating temperatures and ensuring the longevity of dry-type transformers.</p></p>
